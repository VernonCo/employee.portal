Welcome

Welcome to The Vernon Company

Our company was founded in Newton in 1902, by my great-grandfather, F.L. Vernon. We are proud of the success we have achieved over the past century in our 4th generation ‘family’ business, and that we are now one of the largest firms in the Promotional Products Advertising industry.

We sell products and services to business firms in all 50 states through our account representatives and field managers. We have worked hard to achieve an outstanding financial condition (highest Dun & Bradstreet AAAA1 credit rating), pay our suppliers promptly and practice continuous improvement processes throughout our administrative and manufacturing operations.

For us to continue to prosper, we must maintain the best service in the industry and stay on the leading edge of technological advancements. In the final analysis, it’s our “people” who make the difference, and your diligent efforts will help us stay in the lead.

The material in this handbook has been prepared so you have a basic understanding of our policies and procedures. If, however, you have a question at any time, don’t hesitate to contact your immediate manager/supervisor, or Human Resources for clarification.

Again, we hope you have a profitable and exciting career with our company.

Warm regards,
Chris Vernon, President

Code of Conduct

The Vernon Company has been built through the character of its people, and the character is reflected in our Mission & Purpose, Objectives and Business Philosophy. These factors have been fundamental to our success for more than 100 years and will continue to be the basis of our future success. Every employee of The Vernon Company is to act in conformance with this Code of Conduct in all our activities. This Code of Conduct establishes the minimum required guidelines expected of all employees.

Mission & Purpose

The Vernon Company’s mission is to market (and in some cases design, manufacture or import) a broad line of quality promotional products at competitive prices. The products are designed to be safe and provide customers with exceptional value.

The Company will sell on a nation-wide basis through its sales force, while at the same time serving as a supplier to the promotional products and OEM industries as well as special markets through subsidiary operations. Sales in premium, fulfillment, retail and other markets will be pursued as opportunities arise.

The Company will strive for a high standard of excellence and foster the interest of The Vernon Company as a whole.

Objectives

The Vernon Company has five (5) main objectives:

  • Seek to achieve a competitive return on the investment capital employed.
  • Follow a sound growth pattern.
  • Serve customer needs adequately and profitably as well as provide highly acceptable products and customer service commensurate with its profit goals.
  • Strive to be a safe and professional workplace.
  • Demonstrate being a positive corporate citizen.

Business Philosophy

CUSTOMERS

We strive to be recognized by our customers as a company which:

  • Seek to achieve a competitive return on the investment capital employed.
  • Provides exclusive, quality products and prompt delivery at a competitive price.
  • Stands behind its products and services, and maintains a high level of customer satisfaction.
  • Employs honest, loyal and professional individuals.
  • Is an innovator of new products and promotions.

EMPLOYEES

We strive to be recognized by our employees as a company which:

  • Informs employees of their performance as it contributes to objectives.
  • Has high expectations of employee performance and productivity.
  • Bases compensation on individual contribution.
  • Provides opportunity for growth and development within the organization.
  • Expects cooperation, professionalism and quality work for each employee.
  • Values the work of each individual who devotes time, effort and resources to participate in our mission.
  • Treats employees with equity, honesty and dignity.
  • Provides a safe and healthy work environment.
  • Offers competitive wages and benefits in our industry.
  • Provides exceptional sales training in our industry.

COMMUNITY

We strive to be recognized by our community as a company which:

  • Voluntarily contributes time to social, civic and economic responsibilities of a corporation.
  • Encourages employees to participate in the community, while fulfilling Company commitments. This includes devoting leadership to programs in the community.
  • Contributes financially to programs most beneficial to its employees, in a reasonable proportion to other businesses in the community, and to the degree that its earnings permit.

VENDORS & SUPPLIERS

We strive to be recognized by our vendors and industry suppliers as a company which:

  • Is committed to enhancing the growth of the Promotional Advertising Industry.
  • Demonstrates high ethical standards.
  • Expects to buy quality product at a reasonable cost.

COMPETITORS

We strive to be recognized by our competitors as a company which:

  • Is an honest, aggressive adversary in our industry.
