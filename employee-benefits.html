Employee Benefits

Health Insurance

Full time employees are eligible after a 60-day waiting period. Wellmark is our current health insurance provider. There are five options for health insurance which include 2 Alliance Select PPO plans (with a national network and both in and out of network benefits), 2 Blue Advantage HMO plans (which is an Iowa only network and does not include Mayo clinic) and an Alliance Select PPO HDHP / HSA offering.

  • Spouse Medical Coverage Statement
  • Enrollment Form
  • Change Coverage Form
  • HSA Enrollment Form

Dental Insurance

Full time employees are eligible after a 60-day waiting period. Delta Dental is our current dental insurance provider. There are three options for voluntary dental insurance which include a preventative plan, catastrophic plan, and comprehensive plan.


Plan Options

  • Dental Plans
  • Health/Dental Insurance Election Form
  • Enrollment Form

Flexible Spending Accounts

Full time employees are eligible to participate in our group flexible spending plan after a 60-day waiting period.  The amount you designate for the year is taken out of your paycheck in equal installments each pay period and placed in a special account. This benefit that allows you to pay for eligible medical expenses on a pre-tax basis (there are also similar accounts for dependent and child-care expenses).


Forms

  • Informational Brochure
  • Flex Enrollment Form
  • Flex Plan Reimbursement Claim Form

Life Insurance


Company Provided

The Vernon Company provides term life insurance and accidental death & dismemberment insurance for full-time employees after 60 days of continuous employment. The face amount of term life insurance and AD&D is the same per employee and is Company paid.

Dependent Life Insurance

Employees also have the option to purchase term life insurance on their dependents. There are 4 plans and are paid by employee.

  • $5,000 spouse, $2,000 child(ren)
  • $10,000 spouse, $4,000 child(ren)
  • $15,000 spouse, $6,000 child(ren)
  • $20,000 spouse, $8,000 child(ren)

Forms

  • Plan Summary (Hourly)
  • Plan Summary (Salaried)
  • Enrollment Form (Hourly)
  • Enrollment Form (Salaried)

Optional Life Insurance

Employees can apply for the lesser of 3 times their basic annual earnings or $100,000 with no underwriting. You also have the option to purchase additional term life insurance in increments of $10,000 to the lesser of 5 times your basic annual earnings, or a maximum of $250,000 with underwriting for amounts over $100,000.
